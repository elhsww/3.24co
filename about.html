All about our small business:

3.24co started as a dream...

Founded in Sunny Singapore in November of 2020, 3.24co was established to create staple jewelry pieces that centered around anime themes. As an avid anime watcher, owner Chloe Hong wanted good quality, low-key, beatiful accessories to pay homage to her favorite series. What started as a hobby, handcrafting accessories for her friends and family turned into the business we all know and love today!
